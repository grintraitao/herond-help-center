# How to navigate to the cookies setting menu

Accessing the cookies setting menu allows you to manage and control how cookies are handled in your browser. This guide will provide step-by-step instructions on how to navigate to the cookies setting menu in Herond Browser.

## Steps to access cookie settings

1. **Step 1** - Click on the `Menu` icon in the top right, and go to `Settings`.
2. **Step 2** - Select `Additional settings` > `Privacy and Security` > `Cookies and other site data`.

![Cookies setting menu](/img/how-to-navigate-to-the-cookies-setting-menu--file-dRMrHtZQwM.png)

3. **Step 3** - Configuration.

### Cookie default settings

There are 3 different cookie settings: `Allow all cookies`, `Block third-party cookies`, and `Block all cookies`.

`Block third-party cookies` is the default cookie setting. This feature enables the functionality of sites that rely on cookies, such as those using first-party cookies for login purposes. Simultaneously, it blocks ads and third-party cookies from tracking your browsing activity.

### Control cookies with Herond Shield

By simply clicking the blue icon, you can activate Herond Shield and block cookies from tracking your online activities. Enjoy the peace of mind knowing your browsing is secure and private. Best of all, it's free forever!
