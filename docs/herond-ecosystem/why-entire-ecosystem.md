# Why do we have an entire ecosystem and not just the browser?

In this era, solitary products are no longer sufficient. Our focus lies in building a complete ecosystem, despite the lengthy journey it entails. We have already established predetermined milestones, and firmly believe that an interconnected product ecosystem will enable users to unlock the true potential of web 3.0 identity, offering an unparalleled and extraordinary experience with Herond.
