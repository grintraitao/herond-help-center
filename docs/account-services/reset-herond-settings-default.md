# How do I reset Herond settings to default?

You can restore your browser settings in Herond at any time. You might need to do this if apps or extensions you installed changed your settings without your knowledge. Your saved bookmarks and passwords won't be cleared or changed.

1. On your computer, open Herond.
2. At the top right, click `Menu` --> `Settings`.
3. At the bottom, click `Reset Settings`.

:::info
These steps won't provide a full reset. For example, some settings like fonts or accessibility won't be deleted.
:::

## What changes when I restore my settings?

On your Herond profile, the following settings will change to the default:

- Default search engine, which is changed back to Google
- Homepage and tabs
- The new tab page
- Tabs you have pinned
- Content settings, like letting a site show you pop-ups or use your microphone
- Cookies and site data, like items in an online shopping cart
- Extensions and themes

## Troubleshoot reset problems

**Settings menu missing or won't open**

If the settings menu doesn't open or isn't there, Herond might have a problem. To fix, uninstall and reinstall Herond.

## Why did Herond reset my browser settings?

Sometimes, programs that you install can change your Herond settings without you knowing. For your safety, the browser checks if your settings have been changed every time you launch Herond. If Herond notices that something's not right in your settings, it will automatically go back to the original settings.

### Settings that might automatically get reset:

- Default search engine
- Homepage
- Startup pages
- Pinned tabs
- Extensions
