# How do I manage my extensions?

Managing extensions in the Herond Browser allows you to customize and enhance your browsing experience by adding or removing extensions according to your needs. This guide will walk you through the steps to effectively manage extensions within the Herond Browser.

1. **Step 1** - Launch Herond Browser
2. **Step 2** - Go to `Settings` > `Extensions` > `Manage Extensions` from the Herond Browser user interface.
3. **Step 3** - View Installed Extensions
4. **Step 4** - Disable or Enable Extensions
5. **Step 5** - Remove Unwanted Extensions

From a comprehensive list of installed extensions, feel free to effortlessly remove extensions, turn them on and off, or view more details as desired.
