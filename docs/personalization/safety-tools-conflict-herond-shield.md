# Will the safety tools I download cause conflict with Herond Shield?

While you can personalize your browser with extensions, be mindful of their impact. Excessive extensions or conflicting ones can disrupt Herond Shield and overall performance.

With comprehensive ad-blocking, Herond Shield eliminates the need for additional ad-blocking extensions. Keep your browsing experience smooth and efficient by keeping extensions minimal.
