# How do I browse & add an extension?

Explore the Chrome Web Store and enhance your Herond Browser experience! Built on the Chromium platform, Herond Browser allows you to seamlessly browse through the wide array of extensions available. Find the perfect ones to suit your needs and easily add them to your browser.

1. **Step 1** - Go to `Settings` > `Extensions` > `Get more extensions`

![Browse extensions](/img/how-do-i-browse-add-an-extension--file-TliwZaADd9.png)

2. **Step 2** - Click on **Open Web Store**
3. **Step 3** - Choose the extensions from the store and click **Add to Herond**

![Add extension to Herond](/img/how-do-i-browse-add-an-extension--file-jog4ubkhEf.png)

Unlock new functionalities and make your browsing journey even more powerful and personalized. Visit the Chrome Web Store today and discover the possibilities!
