# What technology does Herond Shield use to protect you?

As a unique web 2.5 browser, in the era of decentralization, we understand how our community values privacy. That's why the browser comes with a built-in Herond Shield feature leveraging **SSL certificate**, **Tor integration** and **ASAS (Advanced Security Alert System)** to protect and encrypt user's browsing data, as well as automatically block all pesky ads, allowing users to browse with peace of mind.
