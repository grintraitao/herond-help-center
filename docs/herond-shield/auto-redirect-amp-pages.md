# Auto-redirect AMP pages

- AMP, or [Accelerated Mobile Pages](https://amp.dev/), is a non-standard subset of HTML developed and pushed by Google. AMP pages are served from Google's servers, though designed to look like they're coming from the original publisher's site.
- Herond automatically redirects AMP and similar pages to the publisher's page, which has privacy, security, usability and performance benefits.
- When this feature is enabled, Herond also applies the same protection against [Yandex Turbo Pages](https://yandex.com/dev/turbo/doc/concepts/index.html).
- To toggle this feature, you can go to `Herond://settings/shields` and click `Auto-redirect AMP pages`.
