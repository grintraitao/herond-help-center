# What does ASAS (Advanced Security Alert System) do?

What exactly does ASAS do? In essence, ASAS is a sophisticated security mechanism embedded within Herond Browser that actively monitors your online activities, websites you visit, and files you download. It leverages advanced algorithms and threat intelligence to identify potential risks, vulnerabilities, or suspicious behavior in real-time.

With ASAS, you can rest assured that your online safety is our top priority. The system promptly alerts you about potential security threats, malicious websites, phishing attempts, or suspicious downloads, allowing you to make informed decisions and take necessary precautions to protect your data and privacy.
