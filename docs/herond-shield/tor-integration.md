# What does Tor integration mean?

What exactly does Tor integration mean? Tor, short for The Onion Router, is a renowned network that directs your internet traffic through a series of relays, encrypting it along the way. This layered approach masks your IP address, making it difficult for anyone to trace your online actions back to you. By leveraging Tor's technology, Herond Browser offers a seamless and integrated Tor browsing experience right at your fingertips.

![Tor integration in Herond](/img/herond-shield/what-does-tor-integration-mean--file-0RPPBoXr7K.png)

With Herond Browser's Tor integration, you can access websites anonymously, bypass censorship, and protect your identity from prying eyes. Whether you're concerned about privacy, security, or simply wish to explore the web without leaving a digital footprint, our Tor integration ensures your online presence remains discreet and untraceable.
