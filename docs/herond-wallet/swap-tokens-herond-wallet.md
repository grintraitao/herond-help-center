# How do I swap tokens in Herond Wallet?

Herond browser integrates the Swap feature, using APIs from DEX aggregators such as 0x and Jupiter. From there, we provide a solution for exchanging Tokens with each other with low price spreads and low fees.

To swap from token A to token B, perform the following steps:

1. **Step 1** -- Login to Herond wallet (herond://wallet/)
2. **Step 2** -- Go to the swap section in the Menu.
3. **Step 3** -- Select the network, token to swap, and token to receive, enter the amount to swap.
4. **Step 4** -- Check that the information is correct and click the Review Send button.
5. **Step 5** -- A popup window will appear in the upper right corner, review the transaction again and click approve.

:::info Notes

- Depending on the network, your transaction will be executed from a few seconds to a few minutes.
- Herond referral fee is currently 0.875%.

:::

If you have any questions or problems during the swap, please contact us via the support bubble in the lower right corner or email [contact@herond.org](mailto:contact@herond.org) directly.
