# How to use Herond Wallet?

Herond Wallet supports various cryptocurrencies and digital assets. You can send, receive, and store your digital assets directly within the wallet. Use the wallet's intuitive interface to manage your assets, check transaction statuses, and monitor your portfolio.

Read more about the articles on how to swap, send and buy cryptocurrencies in our help center.
