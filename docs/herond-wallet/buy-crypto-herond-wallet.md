# How do I buy crypto with Herond Wallet?

This guide will walk you through the process of buying cryptocurrency using the Herond Wallet. Follow the step-by-step instructions below to get started with buying cryptocurrency through the Herond Wallet.

1. **Step 1** -- Log in to Herond Wallet on Herond Browser using your Herond Account.
2. **Step 2** -- Navigate to the "Buy" section.
3. **Step 3** -- Choose the desired cryptocurrency you wish to purchase, enter the amount you want to buy, and select your preferred payment method.
4. **Step 4** -- Click on the Review Order button, a pop-up will appear at the top right corner, click to confirm the transaction details.

Herond Wallet ensures a secure and transparent transaction process, allowing you to swiftly acquire cryptocurrencies and expand your digital asset portfolio.

:::info Notes

- Please note that network-dependent transaction times may vary.
- A referral fee of 0.875% is applicable to Herond transactions.
- For any questions or issues during the token/NFT buying process, feel free to contact our support team through the support bubble in the lower right corner of the wallet interface or via email at [contact@herond.org](mailto:contact@herond.org).

:::
