# How do I send tokens / NFTs with Herond Wallet?

The Herond Wallet provides a seamless and secure solution for sending tokens and non-fungible tokens (NFTs) to other addresses on compatible blockchain networks. This guide will walk you through the step-by-step process of sending tokens or NFTs using the Herond Wallet.

1. **Step 1** -- Within the wallet's intuitive interface, locate the "Send" feature.
2. **Step 2** -- Select the type of asset you wish to send -- whether it's tokens or NFTs.
3. **Step 3** -- Enter the recipient's wallet address.
4. **Step 4** -- Specify the amount or choose the desired NFT.
5. **Step 5** -- Click on the Review Order button, a pop-up will appear at the top right corner, click to confirm the transaction details.

Herond Wallet utilizes blockchain protocols to ensure the delivery of your assets within just a few minutes.

:::info Notes

- Please note that network-dependent transaction times may vary.
- A referral fee of 0.875% is applicable to Herond transactions.
- For any questions or issues during the token/NFT sending process, feel free to contact our support team through the support bubble in the lower right corner of the wallet interface or via email at [contact@herond.org](mailto:contact@herond.org).

:::
