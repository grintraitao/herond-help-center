# What is Account Abstraction?

Account abstraction revolutionizes the way users interact with smart contracts by eliminating the need for external private key-controlled Externally Owned Accounts (EOAs).

With account abstraction, users can seamlessly use smart contract wallets for transactions, streamlining the process and enhancing security.

Say goodbye to traditional EOAs and embrace the future of decentralized transactions with Herond Browser.
