# How to Create Your Wallet on Mobile

## Step 1: Open the Herond App and Access the Wallet

1. Open the Herond app on your phone.
2. Tap the **Wallet** icon on the navigation bar to switch to wallet mode.

![Open Herond Wallet](/img/how-to-create-your-wallet-on-mobile--file-u9orxrjhXB.png)

## Step 2: Log In and Start Wallet Setup

When the wallet onboarding screen appears:

1. Tap **Log in to Herond**.
2. Choose one option:

- **Create Wallet** to set up a new Herond Keyless Wallet
- **Import Wallet** if you already have a seed phrase or private key

Herond will start preparing your Keyless Wallet.

![Log In](/img/how-to-create-your-wallet-on-mobile--file-JJrMkXnGlP.png)

## Step 3: Set Your Device Password

Before entering the wallet, you'll be asked to create a login password.

This password protects your wallet data on this device and is required to unlock it.

:::info Tips for a strong password

- At least 10 characters.
- Mix letters, numbers, and a symbol.
- Avoid simple patterns like "12345678".
- Use a password manager to store it safely.

:::

Enter the password twice, then tap **Create**.

![Set Password](/img/how-to-create-your-wallet-on-mobile--file-EFIxEUeWay.png)

## Step 4: Enable Biometrics (Face ID or Fingerprint)

After setting your password, you can choose to unlock your wallet using biometrics.

You may see one of the following, depending on your device:

- **Face ID** (iPhone / supported Android)
- **Fingerprint** (Android devices with fingerprint sensors)

Choose your option:

- Tap **Use Face ID** or **Use fingerprint**, then confirm with your device
- Or tap **Skip** to continue using password-only login

:::info

Using biometrics is recommended for quicker and more secure access.

:::

![Enable Biometrics](/img/how-to-create-your-wallet-on-mobile--file-yUFAAVLLxw.png)

## Step 5: Wallet Setup in Progress

Herond will finish creating your Keyless Wallet. A loading message will appear: **"Your wallet is setting up..."**

After a few seconds, your wallet will be ready. Tap **Get started** to open your wallet homepage.

![Wallet Ready](/img/how-to-create-your-wallet-on-mobile--file-shOZbN4qzM.png)
